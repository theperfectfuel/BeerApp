Welcome!

Beer recipe app thing is a place to discover new beer recipes, make quick and easy shopping lists for new batches and make some connections with other home brewers.

Start by creating a new recipe or just searching through the existing library of brew recipes already here. When you find a recipe you want to try, create a new shopping list from the link on the recipe.
